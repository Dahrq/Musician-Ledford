Perception

Perception is the third studio album by American hip hop artist NF. It was released on October 6, 2017, through Capitol Records alongside NF's newly launched label NF Real Music. The album was entirely produced by American producers Tommee Profitt and David Garcia, and features a guest performance from American singer Ruelle. The album reached No. 1 on the Billboard 200. Songs on the album include triple platinum US single "Let You Down" as well as double platinum US hit "Lie". Throughout the album, NF raps and sings about his his struggles with fear and anxiety, as well as his battles to find out who he is and who he aims to be.

The Search

The Search The Search is NF’s fourth studio album, following up his Billboard-topping 2017 album Perception. In surprising news to many, The Search beat out Chance the Rapper’s debut album, The Big Day, to debut at number one on the Billboard 200 charts and marks NF’s second consecutive Billboard-topping album. The album features songs such as platinum hits in the US, "The Search" and "When I Grow Up." Throughout this album, NF raps and sings about struggles that come along with fame, as well as the effect that fame has had on his life and mental health.
